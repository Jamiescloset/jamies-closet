The Founder

Jamie Alexa Ashton

Jamie's Closet is the vision of Jamie Alexa Ashton — a New Orleans native with an eye for quality and a deep belief that the best accessories tell a story rather than follow a rule.

Growing up in New Orleans — a city that has always known how to dress — Jamie learned early that style is a language spoken through the details. The watch you choose. The chain at your collar. The bag on your shoulder.

Every piece in the collection is hand-selected for quality, authenticity, and that indefinable quality that separates accessories from jewelry.

Style has no gender. Quality has no compromise. Wear what makes you feel like yourself.
